Who Is PhotonEdge?

PhotonEdge (Beijing Hengdingguang Technology Co., Ltd.) is a leading precision optical components manufacturer based in Beijing, China. For over 15 years, we have been providing high-quality optical lenses, mirrors, windows, prisms, filters, beamsplitters, and polarizing components to customers worldwide.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified Quality

Quality is not just a buzzword at PhotonEdge - it's the foundation of everything we do. Our ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system ensures consistent, reliable products through:

  • Strict incoming material inspection
  • In-process quality monitoring at every production stage
  • Final inspection with full test reports for every order
  • Continuous process improvement based on customer feedback
  • Traceability from raw material to finished product

Complete Product Line

We offer over 75 standard optical component product lines across 13 categories:

  • Optical Lenses - Plano convex, double convex, plano concave, double concave, achromatic doublets, aspheric lenses, cylindrical lenses, ball lenses, rod lenses
  • Optical Mirrors - Dielectric, metallic (Al, Ag, Au), laser line, dichroic
  • Optical Windows - BK7, UV fused silica, sapphire, Ge, ZnSe, CaF2, Si
  • Optical Prisms - Right angle, penta, dove, corner cube, equilateral, wedge
  • Optical Filters - Narrow band, ND, color glass, UV/visible absorption
  • Beamsplitters & Polarizers - Plate, cube, polarizing, waveplates, isolators
  • Optical Mounts & Accessories - Mirror mounts, lens mounts, rotation mounts, posts, translation stages, breadboards
