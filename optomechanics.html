Complete Optomechanical Solutions

PhotonEdge offers comprehensive optomechanical components designed for precision optical systems. Our products ensure stable mounting, accurate positioning, and reliable performance for laboratory and industrial applications.

🛢

Optical Tubes & Housing

Precision-machined tubes and housing structures for optical element containment. Available in standard sizes from 1/2" to 2" diameter with AR/AN thread options.

  • Material: Aluminum alloy, black anodized
  • Standard thread options: SM1, SM05, C-Mount
  • Custom lengths available
🔧

Precision Mounting Brackets

Rigid mounting brackets for securing optical elements. Featuring multi-axis adjustment and lock mechanisms for stable positioning.

  • Multi-axis adjustment screws
  • Mirror and lens mounts
  • Post and base combinations
🔄

Optical Mounts & Adjusters

Fine adjustment mounts with micrometer precision for critical alignment applications. Kinematic designs for repeatability.

  • Kinematic mirror mounts
  • Tip-tilt platforms
  • Translation adjusters
↔

Translation Stages

Precision linear and rotary stages for scanning and positioning applications. Manual and motorized options available.

  • Linear translation stages
  • Rotation stages
  • Goniometer stages
📈

Optical Breadboards

Rigid breadboards and honeycomb structures for vibration-dampened optical tables. Multiple sizes and hole patterns available.

  • Aluminum honeycomb core
  • M6 tapped holes on 25mm grid
  • Custom sizes available
⚙

Custom Integration

OEM custom optomechanical integration solutions. We design and manufacture complete optical assemblies to your specifications.

  • Custom enclosures
  • Multi-component assemblies
  • Prototype to production
