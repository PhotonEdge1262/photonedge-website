Research & Academia

Custom Aspheric Lenses for Laser Spectroscopy Research

Custom Aspheric Lenses - UV Fused Silica
Challenge

A leading physics research laboratory needed custom aspheric lenses for their advanced laser spectroscopy experiment. The project required sub-micron surface accuracy and exceptional wavefront quality to achieve the precision needed for their cutting-edge quantum physics research.

Solution

PhotonEdge's engineering team worked closely with the research team to design and manufacture custom UVFS aspheric lenses. We utilized precision CNC grinding and computer-controlled polishing to achieve surface accuracy of λ/20 at 633nm, with scratch-dig specification of 10-5.

Results

Prototypes delivered in 18 days. Lenses exceeded wavefront requirements, enabling breakthrough measurements in quantum entanglement experiments.

Medical Devices

High-Consistency Windows for Endoscope Production

BK7 Optical Windows - 500 Units
Challenge

A major medical device manufacturer needed 500 high-consistency optical windows for their next-generation endoscopic imaging system. Tolerance uniformity across all units was critical, as even minor variations would affect image quality and diagnostic accuracy.

Solution

PhotonEdge implemented strict statistical process control throughout production. We maintained center thickness tolerance of ±0.01mm and parallelism of <1 arcmin across all 500 units. Each window underwent 100% inspection with interferometric verification.

Results

Zero defects after 100% inspection. Customer reported 15% improvement in endoscope image consistency compared to previous supplier.

Industrial Laser

High-Damage-Threshold Mirrors for Laser Cutting Systems

Dielectric HR Mirrors - ZnSe Substrate
Challenge

An industrial laser equipment manufacturer required high-reflectance mirrors capable of handling 5kW CO2 laser power for their metal cutting systems. Previous suppliers' mirrors suffered from premature laser-induced damage.

Solution

PhotonEdge specified enhanced multilayer dielectric coatings with proprietary preconditioning processes. Our mirrors achieved reflectance >99.9% at 10.6μm with laser damage threshold exceeding 50 kW/cm² under CW operation.

Results

Mirror lifespan increased by 300% compared to previous supplier. Customer reported significant reduction in downtime and maintenance costs.

Research & Spectroscopy

Specialty IR Optics for FTIR Spectrometer Upgrade

ZnSe & CaF2 Optical Components
Challenge

A national research institute needed to upgrade their FTIR spectrometer with custom infrared optics. The project required specialty materials (ZnSe, CaF2) with broadband anti-reflection coatings across 2-20μm wavelength range.

Solution

PhotonEdge supplied a complete set of custom IR optics including collimation lenses, beam splitters, and detector windows. Our proprietary broadband AR coating technology achieved >98% transmission across the entire mid-IR range.

Results

Spectrometer sensitivity improved by 40%. The upgraded system has been used for groundbreaking materials characterization research.
