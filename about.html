Company Profile

Who We Are

Beijing Hengdingguang Technology Co., Ltd. is a professional company specializing in the development, production, and sales of optical components. As the core operator of the PhotonEdge brand, we are committed to providing high-quality precision optical elements and solutions to customers worldwide.

Our product line includes lenses, mirrors, windows, prisms, filters, waveplates, beamsplitters, polarizers, and other optical components, covering various materials such as K9(BK7), fused silica, sapphire, CaF2, Ge, Si, and ZnSe.

Core Advantages

  • Complete product line: lenses, mirrors, windows, prisms, filters, waveplates, beamsplitters, polarizers
  • Premium material selection: K9(BK7), fused silica, ZnSe, Ge, Si, CaF2, sapphire
  • Advanced manufacturing equipment and precision inspection systems
  • Professional technical team with R&D capabilities
  • Flexible customization: bulk stock and OEM services available
