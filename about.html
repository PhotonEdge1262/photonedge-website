About PhotonEdge

Beijing Hengdingguang Technology Co., Ltd. - Your Trusted Partner in Precision Optical Components Since 2008

Your Trusted Optical Partner

Beijing Hengdingguang Technology Co., Ltd. specializes in the R&D, production and technical services of high-precision optical components. Its main products include precision lenses, prisms, optical windows and various customized optical modules, covering the full spectrum from visible light to infrared.

With high-precision processing technology and professional coating capabilities, we provide reliable optical solutions for laser equipment, biomedicine, scientific research, aerospace and other fields. Our overseas brand PhotonEdge serves global customers with strict quality and professional technical standards.

Relying on complete customized services and quality control systems, we provide high-quality products and technical support for research institutions and high-end equipment enterprises at home and abroad, striving to become a trusted professional optical brand in the optoelectronic industry.
