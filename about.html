About Us

Beijing Hengdingguang Technology Co., Ltd.

Beijing Hengdingguang Technology Co., Ltd. is a professional company specializing in R&D, production, and sales of optical components. As the core operator of the PhotonEdge brand, we are committed to providing high-quality precision optical components and solutions to global customers.

Optical Materials

K9 (BK7)

Common optical glass material, widely used for lenses, prisms, windows, and mirror substrates. Refractive index Nd=1.51637.

Quartz

Optical quartz glass, can withstand high temperatures and pressure. Applications: special light sources, optical instruments, optoelectronics, military, semiconductor, fiber optics.

ZnSe

Chemical inert material with high purity. Ideal for high-power CO2 laser optics and FLIR thermal imaging system windows.

Ge

Common infrared optical material with high hardness and good thermal conductivity. Widely used in IR imaging and spectroscopy.

Si

Chemical inert material with excellent transmission in 1-7um and 50-300um ranges. Unique properties among IR materials.

CaF2

Good transmission from UV to IR. Widely used in lasers, IR optics, UV optics, and high-energy detectors.
