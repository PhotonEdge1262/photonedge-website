Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our optical components and services

Ordering & Pricing
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For standard catalog components, we have no minimum order quantity - you can order even a single piece. For custom manufactured optics, MOQ typically starts at 5-10 pieces depending on complexity. Contact us for specific project requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank wire transfers (T/T), PayPal, and major credit cards. For established customers, net-30 payment terms may be available after credit approval. All international orders require payment upfront.

Do you offer quantity discounts?

Yes, tiered pricing is available for volume orders. Typical discount tiers start at 10+ pieces, 50+ pieces, and 100+ pieces. Contact our sales team for a customized quote based on your volume requirements.

Lead Time & Shipping
What is your typical lead time?

Standard in-stock components ship within 3-5 business days. Custom manufactured optics typically take 2-4 weeks depending on specifications and coating requirements. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee - please inquire if you have urgent requirements.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide via DHL, FedEx, and UPS. Shipping costs are calculated based on weight, destination, and service level selected. Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are the responsibility of the customer.

Products & Customization
Can you manufacture custom optical components?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom precision optics including lenses, mirrors, windows, prisms, filters, and more. We can work from your drawings, specifications, or even reverse engineer existing parts. Contact our engineering team to discuss your custom requirements.

What materials do you work with?

We work with BK7 optical glass, UV Fused Silica, Sapphire, CaF2, MgF2, Germanium, Silicon, ZnSe, and various optical crystals. Specialty materials can be sourced upon request.

What coating options are available?

We offer a full range of optical coatings: Anti-Reflection (AR) coatings for VIS, NIR, SWIR, MWIR, LWIR bands; High-Reflection (HR) coatings; Partial reflectors; Broadband coatings; Laser line coatings; and custom coatings for specific wavelength ranges.

What tolerances can you achieve?

Our standard precision includes: surface quality 40-20 scratch-dig, surface accuracy λ/4 @ 633nm, centration < 3 arc minutes. Precision options up to 10-5 scratch-dig, λ/20 surface accuracy, and < 30 arc second centration are available for critical applications.

Quality & Certifications
Are you ISO certified?

Yes, PhotonEdge is ISO 9001:2015 certified. Our quality management system covers all aspects of manufacturing from incoming material inspection to final product testing and shipping verification.

Do you provide test reports?

Yes, we can provide inspection and test reports upon request including interferometer surface maps, spectrophotometer transmission/reflection curves, and dimensional inspection data. Certificates of Compliance (CoC) are provided with every shipment.

Technical Support
Can you help with optical design?

Our engineering team can provide technical support for material selection, coating recommendations, and manufacturability feedback. While we don't offer full optical system design services, we can recommend trusted design partners if needed.

Do you offer free samples?

We can provide sample components for qualification testing, typically at standard pricing with credit applied toward future volume orders. Contact sales to discuss sample requirements for your project.

Returns & Warranty
What is your warranty policy?

All our optical components come with a 12-month warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Products must be stored and handled according to recommended guidelines. If you receive products that do not meet specifications, contact us within 30 days for resolution.

What is your return policy?

Standard catalog items may be returned within 30 days in original, unopened packaging for a refund (less shipping costs), subject to a 15% restocking fee. Custom manufactured products are non-returnable unless they fail to meet agreed specifications.
